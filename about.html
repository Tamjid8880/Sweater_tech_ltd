Get to know us

Sweatertech Ltd. is strategically located on Gacha Road, Gazipur Sadar, Gazipur, Bangladesh, just 18 kilometers from Hazrat Shahjalal International Airport. Spanning an impressive 195,000 square feet, our facility features a modern, architecturally designed building equipped with state-of-the-art infrastructure to support efficient and high-quality production.

Labour Relation

Strong and ethical partnerships with our workforce built on respect and fairness

Harmony in the Workplace

Our success is built on strong, ethical, and respectful relationships with our workforce. We prioritize fair wages, safe working conditions, and employee well-being at every level.

  • Ensured all types of workers benefit as per local laws.
  • Ensured minimum wages and benefits for workers.
  • Pay-slips showing detailed breakdown of entitlements.
  • Free medical consultation available throughout the week.
  • Production floor equipped with First Aid, Fire Extinguishers, etc.
  • Yearly bonuses given for one-year continuous service.
  • Regular attendance and incentive bonuses every month.
  • Holiday allowances for employees working on holidays.

Appointment letters with detailed terms & conditions are provided as per regular laws. We build strong and ethical partnerships with our workforce based on respect and fairness.

Work Health & Safety

Ensuring a safe and healthy working environment for all employees.

Work Hours & Overtime

  • Regular hours: 08:00 to 17:00 (1-hour lunch break)
  • Workweek: 6 days (Friday as weekly holiday)
  • Voluntary overtime: Max 2 hrs/day (52 hrs/month limit)

Workplace Safety Measures

  • ✔ Fire extinguishers, hydrants, and emergency drills
  • ✔ On-site medical facility with doctor & nurse
  • ✔ PPE provided (gloves, masks, ear protection, etc.)
  • ✔ Clearly marked emergency exits & evacuation teams

Worker Welfare & Compliance

  • ✔ Hygienic restrooms, canteen, and clean drinking water
  • ✔ Fair wages & benefits per labor law
  • ✔ Strict prohibition of child & forced labor
  • ✔ Grievance mechanism for confidential complaints

Quality Control

Ensuring high standards at every stage of production.

Inspection Stages

  • ✔ In-Line Check (ILC): Inspections during production stages.
  • ✔ Initial Production Check (IPC): Ensures quality from the start.
  • ✔ Mid-Production Check (MPC): Correcting deviations before full production.
  • ✔ Final Random Inspection (FRI): Unbiased quality check before shipment.

Quality Assurance Team

  • ✔ 15-member QA team monitors compliance.
  • ✔ Follows AQL (Accepted Quality Level) standards.
  • ✔ Regular audits to maintain high production quality.

Fabric & Yarn Testing

  • ✔ Ensures high-quality raw materials to prevent defects.
  • ✔ Eliminates issues before production begins.
